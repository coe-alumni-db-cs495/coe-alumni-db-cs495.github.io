Feature Guide: Profile Creation & Editing

Your profile is your professional identity in the alumni database. This guide covers how to create and maintain your profile information.

Accessing Your Profile

After registering for the first time, you'll be automatically redirected to the Profile page to enter your information. You can access your profile anytime by clicking the Profile link in the navigation menu.

Profile Sections

Your profile is organized into three main sections: Personal Information, Academic Information, and Professional Information.

1. Personal Information

This section contains your basic contact and personal details.

Field Required Description
First Name Yes Your first name
Last Name Yes Your last name
Email Yes Your email address (cannot be changed)
Phone Number No Your contact phone number
City Yes Your current city
State Yes Your current state
Zip Code Yes Your current zip code
Bio No A brief description about yourself
Privacy Note: You control what information you share. Only fill out the fields you're comfortable sharing with other alumni. Required fields are necessary for basic functionality.

2. Academic Information

This section captures your educational background at the University of Alabama.

Field Required Description
Major Yes Your undergraduate major (select from dropdown)
Undergraduate Graduation Year Yes Year you graduated with your bachelor's degree
Minor No Your undergraduate minor (select from dropdown)
Master's Degree No Your master's degree (select from dropdown)
Master's Graduation Year No Year you graduated with your master's degree
Doctoral Degree No Your doctoral degree (select from dropdown)
Doctoral Graduation Year No Year you graduated with your doctoral degree
Clubs No University clubs and organizations you were part of
Adding Clubs

When editing your profile, you can add clubs you participated in:

  1. Click the Edit Profile button
  2. Scroll to the Clubs section under Academic Information
  3. Use the club selector to search and add clubs
  4. Engineering clubs are marked with a special badge
  5. Click Save All Changes when done

3. Professional Information

This section highlights your current professional status.

Field Required Description
Company No Your current employer
Job Title No Your current position or role
Certifications No Professional certifications you hold
Adding Certifications

When editing your profile, you can add professional certifications:

  1. Click the Edit Profile button
  2. Scroll to the Certifications section under Professional Information
  3. Use the certification selector to search and add certifications
  4. Click Save All Changes when done

Creating Your Profile (First Time)

When you register for the first time, you'll be automatically taken to the Profile page in edit mode. Here's what to do:

  1. Fill in all required fields (marked as required in the tables above)
  2. Add optional information you're comfortable sharing
  3. Select your major from the dropdown (required)
  4. Enter your undergraduate graduation year (required)
  5. Add any clubs, certifications, or other optional information
  6. Click Save All Changes to create your profile
Important: You must select a major before saving your profile. This is required for the system to create your profile successfully.

Editing Your Profile

You can update your profile information at any time.

Edit Mode Steps

  1. Navigate to your Profile page
  2. Click the Edit Profile button
  3. Make your desired changes to any fields
  4. Update clubs or certifications as needed
  5. Click Save All Changes to save your updates
  6. Or click Cancel to discard changes

What You Can't Change

  • Email Address: Your email cannot be changed after registration. This is your unique identifier in the system.

View Mode vs Edit Mode

View Mode

When not editing, your profile displays as read-only:

  • All fields are displayed but cannot be changed
  • Clubs and certifications appear as simple lists
  • An Edit Profile button is available at the bottom

Edit Mode

When editing, the interface changes:

  • Text fields become editable
  • Dropdowns become selectable
  • Clubs and certifications show interactive selectors
  • Save All Changes and Cancel buttons appear

Profile Visibility

Your profile information is visible to other registered alumni when they search for connections. Other alumni can view:

  • Your name, location, and bio
  • Your academic information (major, graduation year, clubs)
  • Your professional information (company, job title, certifications)
  • Any other information you've chosen to fill out
Privacy Control: The best way to control your privacy is to only fill out the information you're comfortable sharing. Leave optional fields blank if you prefer not to share that information.

How Profile Links to Other Features

Your profile information is used throughout the application:

  • Search: Other alumni can find you based on your profile information (major, company, clubs, etc.)
  • Favorites: When someone views your profile from search results, they can add you to their favorites list
  • Networking: Your profile appears in search results and can be viewed by other alumni looking to connect

Tips for a Great Profile

  • Keep your profile up to date, especially your company and job title
  • Add clubs you were part of - this helps alumni find you based on shared experiences
  • Include professional certifications to showcase your expertise
  • Write a brief, professional bio that highlights your interests or career path
  • Fill out as much information as you're comfortable with to make networking easier

Next Steps

Once your profile is complete, you're ready to start searching for and connecting with fellow alumni.
