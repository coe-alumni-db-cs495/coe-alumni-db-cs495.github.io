Feature Guide: Favorites

The Favorites feature allows you to save alumni profiles for quick access later. Build your personal network of connections and manage them easily in one place.

What Are Favorites?

Favorites are alumni profiles you've bookmarked for easy reference. Think of it as your personal contact list within the alumni database. There's no limit to how many alumni you can favorite.

Accessing Your Favorites

Navigate to the Favorites page from the main menu. You'll see all the alumni profiles you've saved, along with search and sorting tools.

Adding Alumni to Favorites

You can add alumni to your favorites from the Networking page.

How to Add a Favorite

  1. Go to the Networking page
  2. Search for alumni using the search bar or filters
  3. Find a profile you want to save
  4. Click the bookmark icon on their profile card
  5. The icon fills in to confirm it's been added to favorites
Instant Save: Favorites are saved immediately when you click the bookmark button. No additional confirmation is needed.

Viewing Your Favorites

The Favorites page displays all your saved alumni in a tiled grid layout, similar to the Networking page.

Page Header Information

At the top of the page, you'll see:

  • Title: "Your Favorites"
  • Count: Number of saved profiles (e.g., "12 saved accounts")
  • Search Bar: Search within your favorites
  • Sort Dropdown: Change the order of displayed favorites

Profile Card Information

Each favorite card displays:

  • Name: Full name with initials in a colored circle
  • Company & Location: Current employer and city, state
  • Academic Info: Major, minor, and undergraduate graduation year
  • Tags: Badges showing clubs and certifications
  • Remove Button: Red bookmark icon to remove from favorites

Searching Your Favorites

Use the search bar to quickly find specific alumni in your favorites list.

What You Can Search

The search looks through:

  • Names
  • Companies
  • Cities and states
  • Clubs
  • Certifications

How to Search

  1. Type your search term in the search bar at the top
  2. Results filter automatically as you type
  3. Clear the search bar to see all favorites again

Sorting Your Favorites

Use the sort dropdown to organize your favorites list.

Sort Options

Sort Option Description
Name A–Z Alphabetical by first name, ascending
Name Z–A Alphabetical by first name, descending
Company A–Z Alphabetical by company name, ascending
Company Z–A Alphabetical by company name, descending

Viewing Full Profiles

Click on any favorite card to view the complete profile.

Steps to View

  1. Click anywhere on a favorite's profile card
  2. A modal (popup) opens showing their full profile
  3. View all personal, academic, and professional information
  4. Click outside the modal or the close button to return to your favorites

Removing Favorites

You can remove alumni from your favorites list at any time.

How to Remove

  1. Find the profile you want to remove from your favorites
  2. Click the red bookmark icon (filled in)
  3. The profile is immediately removed from your favorites list
  4. The card disappears from the page
Note: Removing someone from favorites is instant and cannot be undone. You can re-add them by finding their profile on the Networking page again.

Empty Favorites List

If you haven't added any favorites yet, you'll see a message prompting you to explore alumni.

What You'll See

  • Message: "No favorites yet"
  • Description: Explanation of how to use favorites
  • Button: "Explore Alumni" button linking to the Networking page

How Favorites Links to Other Features

Networking Connection

Favorites are added from the Networking page. Use search and filters to discover alumni, then save interesting profiles to your favorites.

Profile Connection

The information displayed on favorite cards comes from each alumni's Profile. If an alumnus updates their profile, those changes will be reflected on your favorites list.

Use Cases for Favorites

Building Your Network

Save alumni you'd like to connect with professionally. Organize your networking contacts in one place.

Tracking Classmates

Favorite alumni from your graduation year or major to keep track of where classmates are working.

Finding Mentors

Save profiles of alumni in your desired career field or at companies you're interested in.

Regional Connections

Favorite alumni in your area or a city you're planning to move to.

Tips for Managing Favorites

  • Add liberally: There's no limit, so save any profile that interests you
  • Use search: As your favorites list grows, use the search function to find specific people quickly
  • Sort strategically: Sort by company to see all favorites at the same organization together
  • Review regularly: Periodically review your favorites to see updates to their profiles
  • Remove outdated: Clean up your list by removing connections that are no longer relevant

Favorites vs Networking

Feature Networking Favorites
Purpose Discover new alumni Manage saved alumni
Content All alumni in database Only your saved profiles
Search Advanced filters available Simple keyword search
Sorting Results sorted by relevance Multiple sort options
Action Add to favorites Remove from favorites

Privacy Note

When you favorite someone's profile, they are not notified. Favorites are private to your account - other users cannot see who you've favorited.
