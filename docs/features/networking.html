Feature Guide: Networking

The Networking feature allows you to search for and connect with fellow alumni. Use powerful search and filtering tools to find alumni based on name, company, location, academic background, and more.

Accessing the Networking Page

Navigate to the Networking page from the main menu. You'll see a search bar at the top and a grid of alumni profiles below.

Basic Search

The search bar allows you to quickly find alumni by entering keywords.

What You Can Search For

The basic search looks through multiple fields:

  • Names: First name and last name
  • Companies: Current employer
  • Job Titles: Current position

How to Search

  1. Type your search term in the search bar (e.g., "Microsoft" or "John")
  2. Press Enter or click the search icon
  3. Results appear below in a tiled grid layout

Advanced Filters

Click the filter icon next to the search bar to access advanced filtering options.

Available Filters

1. Clubs

Filter by university clubs and organizations alumni were part of.

  • Click the Clubs section to expand
  • Search for specific clubs or browse the list
  • Select one or multiple clubs
  • Engineering clubs are highlighted
2. Certifications

Find alumni with specific professional certifications.

  • Click the Certifications section to expand
  • Search for specific certifications
  • Select one or multiple certifications
3. Location

Filter by where alumni currently live.

  • Click the Location section to expand
  • Search for a city or state
  • Select a location from the results
4. Undergraduate

Filter by undergraduate academic information.

  • Click the Undergraduate section to expand
  • Major: Select from dropdown of majors
  • Minor: Select from dropdown of minors (optional)
  • Graduation Year: Enter a specific year
5. Graduate

Filter by master's degree information.

  • Click the Graduate section to expand
  • Master's Degree: Select from dropdown
  • Graduation Year: Enter a specific year
6. Doctorate

Filter by doctoral degree information.

  • Click the Doctorate section to expand
  • Doctoral Degree: Select from dropdown
  • Graduation Year: Enter a specific year

Combining Filters

You can combine multiple filters to narrow your search:

  • Use the search bar AND filters together
  • Select multiple clubs or certifications
  • Combine academic filters (e.g., Computer Science major graduating in 2015)
  • All selected filters work together (AND logic)

Search Results

Search results appear in a tiled grid layout showing key information about each alumnus.

Profile Card Information

Each card displays:

  • Name: Full name with initials in a colored circle
  • Company & Location: Current employer and city, state
  • Academic Info: Major, minor, and undergraduate graduation year
  • Tags: Badges showing clubs and certifications
  • Bookmark Icon: Button to add/remove from favorites

Interacting with Results

View Full Profile
  1. Click anywhere on a profile card
  2. A modal (popup) opens showing the complete profile
  3. View all personal, academic, and professional information
  4. Click outside the modal or the close button to return to results
Add to Favorites
  1. Click the bookmark icon on a profile card
  2. The icon fills in to show it's been favorited
  3. The alumni is added to your Favorites list
  4. Click again to remove from favorites

Example Searches

Example 1: Find classmates from your graduation year

  1. Click the filter icon
  2. Expand Undergraduate
  3. Select your major
  4. Enter your graduation year
  5. View results of alumni who graduated with the same major and year

Example 2: Find alumni at a specific company

  1. Type the company name in the search bar (e.g., "Google")
  2. Press Enter
  3. View all alumni currently working at that company

Example 3: Find alumni in your area

  1. Click the filter icon
  2. Expand Location
  3. Search for your city
  4. Select your location
  5. View alumni living nearby

Example 4: Find alumni from the same clubs

  1. Click the filter icon
  2. Expand Clubs
  3. Search for and select clubs you were part of
  4. View alumni who shared the same club experiences

How Networking Links to Other Features

Profile Connection

The information displayed in search results comes from each alumni's Profile. Keep your own profile up to date so others can find you easily.

Favorites Connection

When you find interesting profiles, add them to your Favorites for quick access later. The bookmark icon on each card lets you save alumni profiles instantly.

Search Tips

  • Start broad: Begin with general searches, then narrow with filters
  • Try different terms: If searching for a person, try first name, last name, or both
  • Use filters strategically: Combine location with academic info to find nearby classmates
  • Browse tags: Look at clubs and certifications on cards to discover common interests
  • Favorite as you go: Add interesting profiles to favorites rather than trying to remember them

Next Steps

After finding alumni you'd like to connect with, add them to your Favorites list for easy access.
