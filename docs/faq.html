Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the COE Alumni Database. Questions are organized by feature.

Register & Sign In

Why can't I register with my email?

  • Email already registered: This email is already associated with an account. Use the sign-in page instead
  • Invalid email format: Make sure you're entering a valid email address (e.g., user@example.com)

Why can't I sign in?

  • Incorrect email or password: Double-check your credentials. Passwords are case-sensitive
  • Account not found: Make sure you've registered first, or check for typos in your email address
  • Session expired: If you've been signed out automatically, simply sign in again

Profile

Why can't I save my profile?

  • Major not selected: You must select a major before saving
  • Required fields empty: Fill in all required fields (first name, last name, city, state, zip code, major, graduation year)
  • Invalid data: Check that zip codes are numeric and years are valid

Why aren't my profile changes appearing?

  • Make sure you clicked Save All Changes instead of Cancel
  • Refresh the page to see your updated information
  • If still not appearing, try editing and saving again

Why can't I add clubs or certifications?

  • Make sure you're in edit mode (click Edit Profile)
  • Use the search function in the selector to find clubs/certifications
  • If you can't find a club or certification, it may not be in the system yet

Networking

Why am I not getting any search results?

  • Too many filters: Try removing some filters to broaden your search
  • Spelling errors: Check that names and companies are spelled correctly
  • No matching alumni: The specific combination you're searching for may not exist in the database

Why isn't the search working?

  • Make sure you're entering at least one search term or selecting at least one filter
  • Press Enter or click the search icon after typing
  • Try refreshing the page if results don't appear

Favorites

Why isn't my favorite saving?

  • Make sure you're signed in to your account
  • Check that the bookmark icon fills in after clicking
  • Refresh the Favorites page to verify it was added
  • Try adding again if it didn't save the first time

Why can't I remove a favorite?

  • Click directly on the red bookmark icon, not elsewhere on the card
  • The card should disappear immediately when removed
  • Refresh the page if the card doesn't disappear
